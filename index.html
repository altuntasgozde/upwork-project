Unlike your permanent residence, you may not always be at your summer cottage. Maybe you head to there for the summer or can only drive up every weekend when it’s cottage season or during extended holidays. Still, the property is often left unattended during the workweek, or for long stretches between the freezing winter and the first sign of spring. So how do you secure your real estate investment and preserve the home-away-from-home feeling in between cottage trips?

Just like a standard homeowner’s policy, seasonal home insurance protects your cabin in the woods, lakehouse, or summer cottage — especially when you’re not there for extended periods. Seasonal home insurance helps secure your investment property from losses and damages due to natural events and accidents.
